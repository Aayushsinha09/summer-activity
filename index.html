Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups is this program for?

Our Summer Brain Boost programs are designed for students from Grade 6 to Grade 12. Specific courses may have narrower grade recommendations, which will be detailed on the course information page.

Are the sessions online or in-person?

We offer both online and limited in-person sessions for Summer 2025. Please check the details for each specific course. All online sessions are live and interactive, led by our expert instructors.

What is the duration of the courses?

Most courses run for 4-6 weeks, with sessions typically held 2-3 times per week. The exact duration and schedule will vary by course. Detailed information is available on individual course pages.

How do I register for a course?

You can register by clicking the "Register for Info Session" or "Register Here" buttons on this page, which will take you to our Google Form. For direct course registration (once open), links will be provided, or you can contact us.
